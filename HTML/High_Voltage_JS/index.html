*============*
| High Voltage!!! |
*============*


You are within the walls of a high voltage maze. There are 15 security machines (interceptors) trying to destroy you.

You are the '*', and the interceptors are the '+'. The areas marked 'x' are high voltage! Your only hope is to destroy the interceptors by running them into an 'x'. Your moves are as follows:

7.8.9 ^
4. .6 < + >
1.2.3 v

5 = No move for one turn.
10 = No move for the rest of the game.
-1 = Give up. Situation hopeless.
and of course '0' is super jump.
